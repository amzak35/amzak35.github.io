About me

I’m a Data engineer, BI Consultant, Cyber security and Linux enthusiast, I love and support Free & Open-source software.

I’ve been a self taught “Hacker” my whole life, tinkering with technology and stuff. The internet has always been my source of knowledge, therefore I’m making it my purpose and goal to give back to the community (to the best and limited abilities).

So, this website’s aim is to provide various tutorials on tech, but more importantly, to raise awareness on Free & Open-source software, and help bring non technical people to understand computers and the way they’re supposed to serve us (our needs).

Happy Hacking.
